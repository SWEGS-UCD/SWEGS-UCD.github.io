Chopped

Chopped is a Food Network game show where the participants are tasked with making gourmet dishes incorporating surprise ingredients, which tend to be wacky and unconventional. SWEGS invites EWR students to join us in a recreation of this creative cooking competition on February 8th at 8pm. Secret ingredients will be delivered ahead of time, and at 8pm participants can log on to the SWEGS Game Night Zoom room and start cooking! For more information, contact mehattaway@ucdavis.edu
